Here at nodebucket we believe that it should be easy to view & manange time sensitive tasks. In a world that's moving faster than ever, we solve the problem of keeping track of what needs to be done each day by allowing you to visualize all of your tasks in one place, with flexible customizations that let you stay organized on your own terms.

When we began this project, we began with taking a hard look at how projects were managed across IT organizations across the country and began identifying problems. Outadated project management & lack of organization were two of the biggest issues we were seeing. Thus comes the birth of nodebucket.

Our developers began work dilligently starting with nodebucket's design. How can we design a system to manage time-sensitive tasks in an elegant, simple way? How can we let the user customize the way they want to stay organized?

These were just a few of the questions we asked our selves before writing the first line of code. As you can see, the customer is and always has been our main focus & drive.

We're always in the business of collecting customer feedback, and we've heard you loud and clear, so we are pleased to announce that nodebucket will officially be supported on mobile devices soon!
